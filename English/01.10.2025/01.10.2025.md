Introduction

NovaTech faces significant challenges, including operational costs raise from rapid growth, an inability to adapt to cloud-based service trends, and internal leadership conflicts. This report analyzes these critical issues and proposes a strategic turnaround plan to restore the company’s competitive edge and financial stability.

Analysis of Problems

NovaTech’s rapid expansion has increased operational costs while simultaneously causing a decline in product quality and project delays. This has led to customer insatisfaction. Furthermore, the company is lagging behind competitors who have successfully used cloud-based services, making NovaTech’s traditional offerings less attractive. Internally, conflicting visions and lack of leading person have created a toxic environment. As a result, employee morale has decreased. These combined factors have led to a significant revenue decline.

Proposed Solutions

- Streamline Operations: Immediately consolidate projects to focus on core competencies. This will reduce unnecessary costs, improve resource usage, and enhance product quality.

- Refocus to Cloud-Based Solutions: Allocate research and development budget to create or acquire cloud-based technologies. This is essential to meet modern market demands and attract new clients.

- Improve Leadership and Communication: Engage a neutral manager to align the leadership team on a unified, clear strategic vision. This will resolve internal conflicts, improve decision-making, and help rebuild employee morale.

Conclusion

In summary, NovaTech must optimize its operations, aggressively invest in cloud technology, and unify its leadership. The implementation of these recommendations is critical for navigating this crisis. I hope correct and rapid decisions will stabilize the company, and reposition NovaTech for future growth.

Introduction and summary

The purpose of this report is to present how globalization influenced the growth of the Taiwan Semiconductor Manufacturing Company (TSMC). It discusses the opportunities globalization has created, such as supply-chain development and international division of labor. The report also highlights challenges including geopolitical risks, reliance on global partners and competition from international semiconductor companies. Finally, recommendations are provided to strengthen TSMC’s resilience in a rapidly changing global environment.

Advantages of globalization

Cooperation with international research institutions and major tech companies such as ASML and Zeiss has made impossible technologies reality. The company is now a global leader in advanced semiconductor manufacturing, including high-end and cutting-edge technologies.

Moreover, globalization has enable TSMC to supply chips to customers worldwide. Broad customer base increases revenue, because Taiwan market itself is not that big.

Challenges of globalization

On the other hand, rising tension between USA and China could interrupt and disrupt supply chains or limit market access. TSMC must balance relationships in competing markets.

Also, companies such as Samsung and Intel are investing in advanced manufacturing. This creates competitive pressure on TSMC to maintain its technological lead.

Findings

After studying financial reports, it was found that the majority of TSMC's revenue comes from international customers, confirming globalization role in business growth.

It was also found that global demand for smaller and faster chips places the company in strong competitive position

From the latest politic news it is known that tension between China and US is rising, creating risks for TSMC's global operations

Recommendations

To reduce risks TSMC should continue production diversification by building new facilities in the US and Europe.

Furthermore there is a need for some local supply to decrease dependency on risky international partners.

In addition to this strong government partnerships could protect operations against future political or trade disruptions.

Conclusion

In this report we have looked at how globalization has boosted TSMC's market reach and made it industry leader. However, there are some risks such as competitive pressure and supply chains disruption. To remain the world's leadership TSMC must strengthen it's resilience, expand global manufacturing and continue investing in advanced technology.
